About PennyWise

About Us

At PennyWise, we believe in empowering individuals with the tools they need to take control of their finances. Our innovative budget tracking system seamlessly integrates with your bank account, helping you monitor income, expenses, and savings with ease.

Our Mission

We aim to provide a secure, efficient, and intelligent financial management solution that enhances budgeting efficiency, prevents overspending, and promotes smarter financial decision-making.

How PennyWise Works

Seamless Bank Integration: Automatically tracks transactions to keep spending aligned with your budget.

AI-Driven Budgeting: Uses smart algorithms to allocate monthly expenses efficiently.

Investment Guidance: Offers data-driven recommendations for mutual funds and other investment options.

Security First: Implements two-step verification and data encryption for privacy protection.

Our Vision

Our goal is to transition PennyWise from a web-based platform to a fully functional mobile application, providing users with real-time notifications and enhanced accessibility.
